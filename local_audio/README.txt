Add your MP3/WAV files here with these exact names to connect them to songs:

📁 Required filenames:
- Bohemian Rhapsody - Queen.mp3
- Imagine - John Lennon.mp3
- Happy Birthday.mp3
- Let It Be - The Beatles.mp3
- Twinkle Twinkle Little Star.mp3

🎯 How it works:
1. Download MP3/WAV files of these songs
2. Rename them to match the filenames above EXACTLY
3. Place them in this folder
4. The karaoke app will automatically play your files instead of Spotify previews

💡 Tip: You can also use other formats like .wav, .ogg, or .m4a

🔧 To add more songs:
1. Edit: blind-karaoke/src/lib/database/songs.json
2. Add your new song with a "local_audio_file" field
3. Add the audio file to this folder with the matching name
